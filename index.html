COST ESTIMATOR TOOL

The Built for Zero team has created a Cost Estimator Tool to support your strategic response to COVID-19. Use this tool to estimate of the need and cost for: Quarantine and Isolation (Q&I) units in the crisis response phase; Prioritized permanent housing placements for a high risk cohort before a potential second wave of infection in the fall/winter. The tool gives two options to estimate need and cost of: Permanent housing placements for individuals 50+ years of age; and Permanent housing placements for individuals experiencing chronic homelessness.
