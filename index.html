COST ESTIMATOR TOOL

The Built for Zero team, in partnership with Kaiser Permanente, has created a Cost Estimator Tool to support your strategic response to COVID-19.

Use this tool to estimate of the need and cost for:

  • Quarantine and Isolation (Q&I) units in the crisis response phase;
  • Prioritized permanent housing placements for a high risk cohort before a potential second wave of infection in the fall/winter.

For the following populations:

  • All individuals experiencing homelessness
  • Individuals 50+ years of age
  • Individuals experiencing chronic homelessness.
